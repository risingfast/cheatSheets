Cheat Sheets


Choose a sheet name and click submit to see the sheet contents
The 'submit' button will remain disabled until a sheet name is selected
Enter a text filter and click 'Submit' to filter the sheet contents
The filter is case-sensitive and does not support wildcards or regex expressions
The filter allows multiple words and spaces

Click 'Wrap' to word-wrap or turn off word-wrap on the displayed sheet contents
